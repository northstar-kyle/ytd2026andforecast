How it works. Each pursuit is weighted by a close probability, then split across Q3 and Q4. Opportunities are timed by stage (later stage closes sooner); the eight open CRM Deals use their actual estimated close dates. Swing factors (≥ $50M) are shown individually because each is large enough to move the number on its own. Base pipeline (under $50M) is the diversified, more reliable layer.
